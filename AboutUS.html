CLIMATE

In Barrie, summers are pleasant, long, and warm. This time of the year, tourists prefer to visit this city as it has a lot of beaches. It starts from May to September. During those times average temperature varies from 20.1 C to 26.0 C.

Autumn stays for October and November. Fall time is the best time of the year as comparatively to other season as it contains wonderful colors. People starts decorating their houses for Halloween. City looks so pretty in these times.

Winters starts from December and stays till march here. Temperature goes to -26C. Average Snowfall goes up to 80.2cm. These times people prefer to do fun activities like skiing. City looks so pretty with Christmas and New years decoration. People enjoy their holiday season.

Spring stays for two months that is April and may when people feel the beauty of blooming flowers. Average temperature for this season starts from 10.6C to 18.1C.
